### 5\. Rode NT1-A

![rode nt1 a](https://images.wondershare.com/filmora/article-images/2022/09/asmr-microphones-5.jpg)

![sennheiser mkh416](https://images.wondershare.com/filmora/article-images/2022/09/asmr-microphones-7.jpg)
